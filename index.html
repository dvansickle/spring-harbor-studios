Small, useful web tools for specific interests

Spring Harbor Studios creates small, fun web tools that make daily life a bit simpler and more enjoyable. The focus is on light, practical ideas—things that are easy to use, helpful in everyday situations, and made with a clear point of view. We build for small groups with special interests rather than trying to make one thing for everyone.

About

Spring Harbor Studios is an independent developer making straightforward utilities for the web. Our projects are intentionally compact, easy to set up, and respectful of your privacy.

  • Developer: Spring Harbor Studios, Madison, WI (USA)
  • Contact: support@springharborstudios.com

Our Tools

Google Calendar add-on

Windblock

Monitors wind forecasts for your ZIP code and blocks off hours that meet your threshold on a dedicated “Windblock” calendar. If the wind drops, the time is freed. Runs within your Google account; no personal data sold or shared.

  • Creates and updates a private calendar in your account
  • Uses public weather data (Open-Meteo)
  • Support: support@springharborstudios.com

Chrome extension

YearMap

A year-at-a-glance planner inspired by the Neatnik calendar. Quickly block ranges of days, tag them with colors, and export to ICS for your calendar.

  • Local storage for your settings
  • Optional ICS export
  • Support: support@springharborstudios.com
